About Sphere Consturctions


Investigating since 2010

The first spark looks like it will start the fire, but it bursts brightly and then immediately fizzles out. It's the second spark that burns hot enough and long enough for the kindle to catch fire.
Our clients are the first sparks who try and solve the cases by themselves, before burning themselves out quickly without achieving any results. Our investigators are the Second Sparks that will solve, find and discover what our clients weren't able to discover on their own.
Our Second Sparks are competent, discreet and highly trained. They always succeed and find the solution to your problem, whatever it is.

Our main office is based in Nova Scotia (Canada) but we have discreet investigators in many cities all over the world.

Our Investigators


CEO and Founder

Annar Neisti founded the company in Iceland in 2007 and relocated to a small town in Nova Scotia in Canada a few years later. He supervises the recruitment of new Sparks and talks to clients when they first come to us. You can contact him at annar.neisti@secondsparks.com


Investigators

Our investigators are the Sparks that carry out the investigations. They are located all around the world. Their exact number is known only to our CEO and they have never met each other. They are impossible to spot but carry an official badge for identification when needed.


New Recruits

We are always looking for more Sparks to join our team. When we find someone with exceptional skills we test them and if they are good enough they become one of us. We do not accept applications: when we hear that someone is likely to meet our very high standards of competence we contact them directly.
